Mybit

Easy tool to make school volunteer sign ups manageable

The goal of Mybit is to give a busy parent the ease to go online to organize his/her schedule and enage more with kids and their schools.

How Mybit works



Create Event

A school parent can volunteer as an admin and create, monitor and publish all the upcoming events at a school.

Register for Events

It allows other parents to sign up for a volunteer activity at school, keep track of their volunteer activity profile.

Get Reminders for Events

Organize your engagegment with kids school events with ease by getting regular reminders.

Share feedback

Share feedback with other parents about an event they volunteered for with a feedback survey.
